Drawing and Painting

My artistic talent is one of my favorite traits of myself. It provides an outlet for my creativity and imagination, and the process of creating something is enjoyable. I love how I can do whatever I want when I want to create something; it gives me the ability to fully realize my imagination. Crocheting

Crocheting is a hobby I recently picked up, and I have since become obsessed with it. The rhythmic pace at which you create each knot in a crochet pattern is therapeutic and satisfying (not to mention it is fairly easy to learn). I love crocheting gifts for others, as well as clothes for my plushies.

Cooking and Baking

As a foodie and someone who loves to eat, I love to cook and bake. I especially enjoy cooking or baking with loved ones, such as my mom and my friends. Some of my favorite and nostalgic memories come from cooking or baking with my loved ones, like when I would try to help my mom in the kitchen as a little kid. I believe that cooking or baking your own food makes it taste so much better, and doing it with people you love makes invaluable memories.

Volleyball

I started playing volleyball in middle school, and I immediately fell in love with the sport. I enjoy the triumph I feel whenever we win (even the small wins), the lessons I learn from my mistakes, the thrill I feel when I play, and I love the people and teammates I have met and formed a camaradie with over the years. Volleyball has taught me invaluable lessons, such as leadership and teamwork, and given me priceless memories.

Piano

I love playing the piano. The best part is when you finally master a song after weeks of continuous practice and hard work. I also enjoy learning new songs and adding them to my repertoire. Although recitals used to be nerve-racking for me, I have since grown to enjoy performing in front of others.

Traveling

Traveling is by far one of my favorite things to do. I love visiting places to learn about the culture, enjoy the food, shop, relax, and spend time with my loved ones. My favorite place that I have visited is my motherland, Vietnam. Some of my favorite memories are from places I've traveled to. I simply just can't think of a time where I went on vacation and wasn't happy .
